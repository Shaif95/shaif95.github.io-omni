About Omni.ai

Omni.ai is an open-source, crowd-sourced data platform aimed at providing accessible, ethically sourced datasets and training infrastructure for advancing AI foundation models. Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality data and empower the AI community.

We focus on ensuring compliance with industry standards, providing a secure and privacy-respecting infrastructure for data hosting and annotation. Join us in unlocking the next wave of AI innovation!

Collaboration with Open Data Initiatives

We collaborate with prominent open data sources, including:

  • CERN Open Data - Access to datasets from the Large Hadron Collider experiments.
  • LIGO Open Data - Open data from gravitational wave observations.
  • BINGO - Datasets related to cosmic evolution and early universe studies.
